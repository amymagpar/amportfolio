AI chat assistant for Meraki Marketplace

Understanding the Problem

User research revealed that developers had to apply multiple filters before finding an API that met their business needs. Their searches were highly specific, requiring significant effort to identify relevant matches.

Meraki Marketplace AI search

Designing an AI-Powered Solution

To streamline this process, I leveraged Cisco’s AI assistant to design a GenAI-powered chatbot and search tool for the Meraki Marketplace. This solution aimed to help developers quickly and intuitively find APIs that aligned with their requirements. I developed common user flows and intents based on search behavior analytics and session replays, ensuring the AI assistant could anticipate needs and provide relevant, high-quality recommendations.

 

Educating the Team on AI Implementation

To align stakeholders across sales, engineering, and product, I created an AI primer outlining the costs, challenges, and opportunities of integrating AI into the marketplace. This resource enabled cross-functional teams to make informed decisions and ensured a shared understanding of the AI-driven enhancements.

Experimenting with AI for Personalized User Engagement

Beyond my work at Cisco, I explored AI’s potential by designing a custom chatbot using Google’s Dialogflow CX. By integrating my resume, case studies, and website content, I built an interactive experience that allowed hiring managers to learn about my expertise through a conversational interface, showcasing AI’s ability to enhance user engagement and accessibility.
