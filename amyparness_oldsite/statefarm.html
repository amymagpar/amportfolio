State Farm

Business Goals

duce insurance premiums based on your driving.
Create a self-service model for all things insurance.

Challenges

The size of the site made it important to all follow a standardized format for all of our deliverables.
Allowing an insurance company access to your data is scary.

Solutions

Offer a large discount in the beginning. Make enrollment very simple.
Make everything clear and give all of the information they need to make a decision without an agent. Offer live chat and a customer service number at all times.

Role

I lead a team of copywriters and art directors to create the new product Drive Safe and Save and then went on to work on self service with a focus on auto insurance.
Heuristic evaluations, use case documentation, user journeys, flow diagrams, interface design, effort level rating for dev team

Impact

Increase in Drive Safe & Save enrollment. The self-service experiences on the .com decreased support costs by 30%.

Research, test & iterate

Recommendations came out of a competitive and functional analysis and a heuristic evaluation. Print outs were pinned up all over the room to be updated as changes were made. Team meetings were held daily.

Self-service

State Farm began to use AI to better serve its customers. Things that impact customer satisfaction like filing a claim and billing needed to be painless and transparent. We made these tasks automated with options to chat with an agent. These tasks were brought out on the homepage and made front and center in the auto insurance section. The self-service experiences on the .com decreased support costs by 30%.

Connected Devices Mitigate risk

Telematics use GPS and on-board diagnostics to provide insurance companies with actionable insights to more accurately assess risk, price premiums, service claims and reduce fraud. Telematics was just beginning to be used to rate insurers and give discounts. It was my job to figure out how to present this new opportunity in a customer-friendly way. Although it does have a better business strategy than using one's credit scores to determine rate, it does have a lot of negative privacy implications for the customer. To solve this, we offered an easier opt-in and a 50% discount at first.
