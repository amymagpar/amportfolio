AI chat assistant for Meraki Marketplace

User research showed that developers would need to filter results several times before arriving at an API that might meant their business need. They searched very specific criteria and this required more effort to find a potential match.

Designing a GenAI chatbot and search tool to utilize Cisco's AI assistant to find APIs for their business more quickly was our goal. I created some common flows and intents based on search results and session replays.
