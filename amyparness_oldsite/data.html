Data & Analytics: Telling a Story

Decreasing churn and increasing loyalty?

In this project, we began by looking at an overview of the big data using Google Analytics with Little Data, Tresl and Gorgias. We created cohorts based on the larger data view to narrow down our pool and get more specific information and feedback.

Survey Design

I led the team in creating surveys in TypeForm and sending them out to these cohorts.

Survey data from thousands of respondents was analyzed and then used to create 1:1 customer interviews. Personas and Journey maps were created to focus on our goals.

What is the typical user journey after purchase?

Our team analyzed products that were typically bought together. This allowed us to make decisions on ad campaigns and promos we would send to loyal customers.

From this analysis, we could also see options to test different cross sells on the product pages.

Conversion tools

100% of our active customers converted within 24 hours of signing up. It means the sign-up discount is a high conversion tool.

Inventory management

Replenishment data helps us see when different cohorts replenish. We could forecast when we would need to have certain stock and how much to order.
