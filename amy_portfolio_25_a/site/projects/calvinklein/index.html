Launched in 18 CK stores + Macy's

Creation of the human factors guides allowed CK to install these screens in as many places as they wanted.

Immersive shopping experience

Calvin Klein installed wall mounted and desktop interactive screens in eighteen of their stores. I was given a creative brief from an agency and the technical specs of the interactive screens to create a Fit Guide for CK Jeans. The video and look and feel was inspired by Calvin Klein's 'white room ads'.

Gesture based interactivity

The scale was 1:1 in order to give viewers a real idea of how the jeans would fit them. Visitors could select any of the looks on the screen from an ambient video role, and see details of the different washes and fit of the jeans.

I had to get people to interact with the screen without audio. I worked closely with the hardware team to create the experience on the 75-inch interactive Samsung wall-mount touch display and a 52-inch tabletop.

Human Factors

We followed best practices for average heights in the USA. We printed mock-ups of the interface and created it on the wall of the studio in order to test some of our measurements with many different sized people.

Challenges

Creating intuitive gesture control of the screen and video. How will customers know they can interact with the screens without audio?

Solutions

Large-scale paper prototypes were tested before creatiing multiple interactive prototypes.

Iterative design process

I created three different gesture-based interface designs that were tested by the team in the office. We needed to ensure the screens were intuitive and allowed for customers to use them as a sales assistant.

Unified Shopping Experience

This unified shopping experience blended physical stores and their online store, allowing customers to purchase online while in-store, browse digital lookbooks, and engage with the brand through the touchscreens.

These virtual sales assistants leveraged Macy's artificial intelligence system to show shoppers where merchandise could be found by synchronizing LED lighting that emphasizes placement on shelves.
