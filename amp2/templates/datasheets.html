Sustainability and design guides

Being on the Sustainability global messaging team allowed me to see the connection between datasheets and their ability to educate and inform customers about the tangible advantages of adopting sustainability. Using Applicable information as well as high-level company goals and vision to express

By highlighting energy-efficient technologies, usage metrics, and environmental benefits, these datasheets can serve as powerful tools to educate and inform customers about the tangible advantages of adopting sustainable solutions as well as meeting government regulations. Incorporating energy management static and later interactive data into datasheets is a pivotal step in promoting the sustainable features and products that Cisco offers.

Lead generation and upgrading

I ensured the datasheets offered multiple touchpoints for lead generation and nurturing. Datasheets inform but also have the potential to drive conversion because they are usually directly searched for and accessed directly by many different types of customers. Previously, marketing ran a promotion on some datasheets that was very successful.

I ensured strategic placement of trials and promotions in meaningful places instead of at the top of the datasheet. This approach enhances the user experience and aligns with our marketing and sales objectives, transforming datasheets into key contributors to growth. The improved design ensures that customers are not only informed but also engaged, driving higher conversion rates and fostering long-term relationships.
