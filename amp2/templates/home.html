Hello!

Most recently, Amy was a UX Leader at Cisco Meraki where she led cross-departmental UX strategies that connected dots between cross-org products  to deliver a cohesive customer experience.

Before Cisco, Amy held UX Director positions at top e-commerce agencies and led product design for start-ups to enterprise level companies like Samsung, State Farm, Sony Ericsson, and Lucid Motors.

Amy started Sparkle Labs in 2005 where she created educational kits to teach electronics and green tech. Her work has been shown at MOMA, Art & Science exhibitions throughout Asia and the USA. She speaks on making, right to repair, and mentors in STEM.
