Sparkle Labs' grew out of a sketchbook full of interactive toy ideas and prototypes created during graduate school. We worked with toy companies like Mattel, KidRobot and Hasbro. After writing a few articles for Make Magazine, we realized readers did not understand the basics of electronics.

We now have seven kits that teaches both kids and adults how to prototype and understand electronics. Working with electronic circuits grows problem solving skills, enables the ability to prototype ideas for products and repair all of the electronics that surround us.

Discover Electronics

This is our flagship kit and the beginning of it all. We made the first kits by hand and once demand was great enough, we went to China to meet manufacturers. This has been a learning process where now we have a partnership with our factory.

From the beginning, we worked to make the packaging sustainable and free of plastics. The Discover Electronics kit is in its third version. From surveys and feedback from educators and owners of the kit, we learned that some of them buy plastic containers to store the parts. In the third version, we totally redesigned the packaging to make the kit itself a storage containers made out of pulp paper waste.
