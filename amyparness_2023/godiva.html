Godiva

Business Goals

Create an experience that reflects the culture and special features of the particular property.
Increase AOV (average order values) and loyalty. Allow agents to book on the site using their personalized codes.

Challenges

Godiva wanted an overhaul of the customer experience to create a more unified and luxurious feeling.
They needed to connect the in-store experience to the online shopping experience.

Solutions

Create a boutique experience online. We held workshops with fulfillment and the customer service teams to create greater efficiency using the website.

Role

Managed a small team of UX and design, Heurisitc evaluation, competitive landscape, functional audit, personas, design workshops, sitemaps, wireframes

Impact

What's Inside was a huge game changer for Godiva's online sales.

What's inside?

We created a "Look inside" that allowed customers to see what is inside the box with the cross-section of each piece. This was a major photographic and content creation undertaking that increased sales exponentially. Identifying what was inside with keywords led to an ability to personalize by types of chocolate and tastes like people can do in stores.

When will it get there?

In most cases, gifting requires an exact delivery date. We created a calendar that allowed the gifter to chose which day the package should arrive and get their shipping options and pricing. They could also select flexible free delivery or a future date delivery up to three months in advance.

Educate. Craftsmanship. Heritage.

The content strategy focused on the heritage and craftsmanship of the chocolate. A new section, The Godiva Experience, showcases their award-winning chefs and chocolatiers. In our research, customers were unaware that Godiva creates over 40 new and seasonal recipes a year. We turned their process of creating new flavors into content they could use in print and digital.

Multichannel connectedness

Godiva's chocolate covered strawberries are their most popular product in North America, but they are only sold in a boutique. We gave popular in-store only items space on the website to drive customers into boutiques. We also added a find in store widget to the product pages.
