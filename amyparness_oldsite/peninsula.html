Booking an experience at Peninsula Hotels

Business Goals

Create an experience that reflects the culture and special features of the particular property. Increase AOV (average order values) and loyalty. Allow business and agents to book on the site with codes.

Challenges

Designing a unified luxury experience between the properties and the digital world of Peninsula. Three sites available in 9 languages.

Solutions

Redesign of the booking flow to make it more personalized and smarter. Allow experiences in each property to be booked along with the rooms. Allow suites to be booked online instead of having to call to book.

Role

I directly managed and mentored one UX designer and two Art directors. I led the competitive research, functional audit, collaborated with the business team to create user stories and personalized offerings, designed new features like room layout and compare, wireframes for booking and checkout for a global multilingual site.

27% increase in page views, 18% increase in revenue, 53% increase in conversions
Enhanced user journeys into the booking flow. Improved the booking experience by reducing the number of steps from 8 to 3.

Research

Research was done with the sales and marketing teams in Hong Kong. We did a functional audit, personas and user stories for development.

Booking widget

I created a booking widget that followed the customer from the homepage to reservation confirmation. I also added Make a reservation on key landing pages and on specific rooms to make it easier to get the room and experience in the promo block.

Localization

We designed the site for multiple languages and currencies.

Personalization

Based on analytics and hotel concierge feedback, we saw an opportunity to make the whole experience more personalized and elevated. We also created user accounts that saved information on the customer so we could auto populate or recommend based on their preferences.
