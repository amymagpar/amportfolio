What is a best in class portal?

A great portal includes key features including: personalization and customization options, educational opportunities, delivery tracking, a community forum, and integrated customer support. Interactive tools such as a charging station locator, cost savings calculator, and environmental impact tracker add value.

Seamless integration with a mobile app for on-the-go access and remote vehicle control functions add convenience. Early access to new features is a great way to build brand loyalty and get valuable feedback.

The Vision

Electric vehicles represent a transformative shift in the automotive industry, not only in terms of technology but also in how customers engage with their vehicles. Lucid Motors recognizes that an informed and engaged customer is more likely to appreciate and fully utilize the advanced features of their EV. Therefore, the customer portal was envisioned as a comprehensive, user-centered digital platform that would cater to the unique needs and questions of EV owners at each stage of their journey.

Stages of Ownership

  • Design Phase

    Education: New customers often need to understand the benefits of EV technology, including sustainability, cost savings, and performance improvements.
    The portal provides in-depth resources about Lucid's cutting-edge technology, design philosophy, and customization options.

    Customization: Users can interactively design their vehicle, choosing from various options to create a personalized EV that meets their exact preferences and needs.

  • Waiting for Delivery

    Anticipation: The period between ordering and receiving an EV can be filled with anticipation and excitement. The portal keeps customers engaged with real-time updates on their vehicle's production status, estimated delivery dates, and insights into what to expect upon delivery.

    Preparation: Educational materials prepare customers for the transition to EV ownership, including charging infrastructure, maintenance tips, and tutorials on the vehicleâ€™s features.

  • New Owner

    Onboarding: As new owners receive their vehicles, the portal offers step-by-step guides and video tutorials to help them get acquainted with their EV. This includes setting up their car, understanding the dashboard, and utilizing the advanced driver-assist features.

    Support: Easy access to support services ensures that any initial questions or issues can be quickly addressed, providing peace of mind to new EV owners.

  • Seasoned Owner

    Optimization: For seasoned owners, the portal continues to provide value by offering advanced tips on optimizing vehicle performance, maximizing battery life, and making the most of software updates.

    Community Engagement: Owners can connect with other Lucid EV enthusiasts, share experiences, and participate in exclusive events and forums, fostering a strong community around the brand.

Reservation to ownership: Building habits.

The perks of ownership are teased into the reservation and customization process of the portal. Guest users were invited to the same digital experience as the owners.

Impact

There was a direct correlation between the changes to portal experience and the user to owner conversion rate.
Customer feedback for financing, rebates, and payment was shifted to positive.
Lucid has a top-rated delivery experience due to the implemented service design changes.
